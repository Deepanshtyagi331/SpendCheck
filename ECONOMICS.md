# ECONOMICS.md

## Value of a Lead
A converted lead is worth **$3,000 - $15,000 ARR** to Credex.
- **Reasoning**: If a startup spends $2,000/mo on AI, and Credex sells them discounted credits/infrastructure at a 10-15% margin, we earn ~$300/mo. If we help them optimize and consolidate into a larger enterprise deal via our partnership, our commission/margin increases. High-growth startups also increase spend by 2x-5x annually.

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **X/LinkedIn (Organic)**: $0 (Time-intensive).
- **SEO/Content**: ~$50-100 per high-intent lead (Content production cost).
- **Word of Mouth**: $0 (Viral loop of shared audit links).
- **Target CAC**: < $500 for a qualified consultation booking.

## Profitability Math
To be profitable at a $500 CAC:
- Audit -> Consultation conversion: 10%
- Consultation -> Purchase conversion: 20%
- This means 50 audits = 1 purchase.
- If 50 audits cost $500 to generate ($10/audit), we break even on the first year's margin of one customer.

## Driving $1M ARR in 18 Months
To hit $1M ARR ($83k/mo):
- At $500/mo avg margin per customer, we need ~166 active customers.
- At 2% total conversion (Audit to Purchase), we need 8,300 completed audits.
- 8,300 audits over 18 months = ~15 audits per day.
- **Goal**: Scale traffic to 100 visitors/day to achieve 15-20 audits/day.

## The Viral Engine
If each shared audit link generates 0.2 new audits (K-factor of 0.2), our acquisition cost effectively drops by 20%. If we can push the K-factor to 0.5 through "Benchmark comparisons" (e.g., "You are in the top 10% of efficient AI spenders"), we double our growth rate.
