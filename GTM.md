# GTM.md (Go-To-Market Plan)

## Target User
The "Resourceful CTO" or "Engineering Manager" at a Series A-C startup (15-150 employees). They are currently feeling the "AI subscription tax" — where every new developer adds $20-60/mo to the burn, and API costs are scaling faster than revenue.

## What they Google
- "Cursor vs Copilot enterprise cost"
- "How to save on OpenAI API bills"
- "Claude 3.5 Sonnet vs GPT-4o pricing comparison"
- "Startup AI budget benchmarks"
- "Reducing engineering SaaS spend"

## Where they hang out
- **Subreddits**: r/startups, r/SaaS, r/CTO.
- **Slack Groups**: CTO Connection, Rands Leadership Slack, various local "Startup Hub" Slacks.
- **Discord**: Vercel/Next.js Discord (in the help/showcase channels).
- **X Lists**: "Startup Founders," "Tech Lead Twitter," and following AI influencers like @levelsio or @pwang.

## First 100 Users Strategy ($0 Budget)
1. **The "Benchmark" Post**: Create a data-driven post on LinkedIn/X titled "The Average 50-Person Startup is Overpaying for AI by $800/mo." Link to the tool as the "benchmark" source.
2. **Community Value-Add**: Find "How do I save on API?" questions on StackOverflow and Reddit. Provide a detailed answer about prompt caching and then link to SpendCheck for a full audit.
3. **Founder Outreach**: DM 20 founders of recently funded startups on X. "Hey [Name], noticed you're scaling fast. We built a tool to help founders audit their AI spend (Cursor/Claude/etc). Takes 60s, free forever. [Link]"
4. **Product Hunt Launch**: Launch with a "Credex Audit" hook. Position it as a gift to the community.

## The Credex Unfair Advantage
Credex already has relationships with startups buying credits. We can email our existing "low balance" users with the audit tool as a value-add service. "Before you buy more credits, ensure you aren't leaking money on sub-optimal plans."

## Week-1 Traction Goals
- 500 Unique Visitors
- 150 Completed Audits (30% conversion)
- 45 Leads Captured (30% audit-to-lead conversion)
- 5 Consultation Bookings (10% lead-to-consult conversion)
